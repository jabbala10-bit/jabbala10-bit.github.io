Common questions

What makes this different from hiring an AI engineer? +
Most engineers build what they know, usually wrappers with retry logic. We bring a systems design mindset: orchestration layers, constraint engines, and observability from day one. You get architecture that scales, not a demo that breaks.
Do we need to be technical to work with you? +
No. The audit and architecture tiers are designed for founders and product leads. We explain every decision in plain language. For the full build, we work directly with your existing dev team or independently on your cloud infrastructure.
What is the constraint layer and why does it matter? +
The constraint engine is a runtime verification layer between your agents and the real world. It blocks unsafe actions, validates outputs against policy, and ensures your agents stay within permitted scope as they scale.
What tech stacks do you work with? +
We are stack-agnostic. We have built on LangGraph, AutoGen, custom Python orchestrators, and TypeScript runtimes. We recommend what fits your team and requirements.
How do you handle our data and IP? +
Everything stays in your infrastructure. We never train on your data, we sign NDAs before engagement, and all IP produced during the project is fully yours.
